Hi.

My name is William Drell, and I'm a fan of Mongoose Traveller First Edition.

I created this program as a personal programming challenge while learning Python.

Also, I just wanted to make Traveller characters because I think its fun.

The primary executable will be main.py.  It will draw from config.py, careers.py, and planetgen.py.

careers.py, main.py, and config.py are feature complete in beta status as of 3-18-26.

Anyone wanting to try out this program is quite welcome to, and you are free to use it as a jumpstart
for any of your own projects.

This program is not intended to replace any Mongoose Traveller product and is best used in conjunction with the
Mongoose Traveller First Edition Core Rulebook.  I have made some stylistic changes to the original content, intentionally
ommitted certain features, and compressed a few skills based on my table's preferences.

Notable ommissions are the lack of anagathics and emergency medical services.  Most of the people I've played with
would rather just have a round of laughter and applause at the absurdity of dying in character creation, rather than
playing a character with one or more stats reduced to "1".

Skills like "Pilot" and "Engineer" are not broken into sub-skills, because I figure you can decide what specialties your
character would have based on the needs of your campaign.

Characters created by this program are essentially incomplete, needing finishing touches by you and your GM with the aid
of the Core Rulebook.

Have fun and don't forget your towel.
